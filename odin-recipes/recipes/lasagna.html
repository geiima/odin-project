Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a hearty and flavorful dish, perfect for family gatherings or special occasions. Layered with rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese, it is the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef and season with salt and pepper. Mix in the marinara sauce.
  4. In a baking dish, layer the noodles, meat sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers.
  5. Top with Parmesan cheese and cover with foil.
  6. Bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for another 10 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
  7. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
