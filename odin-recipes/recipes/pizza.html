Pizza

Description

This classic Margherita pizza is a simple yet delicious Italian dish. It features a crispy crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves. It’s the perfect recipe for pizza lovers who enjoy authentic flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface and transfer it to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with slices of mozzarella cheese and fresh basil leaves.
  5. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve.
